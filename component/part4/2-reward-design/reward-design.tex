\subsection{Reward Design}
\label{sec:reward-design}
\nestindent

We design five auxiliary reward functions to address the failure modes identified in Section~1.2.
All five are rule-based with no learned components, following DeepSeek-R1's~\cite{deepseekr1} principle that verifiable rewards resist reward hacking better than neural reward models.
Each reward maps a response to a scalar in $[0, 1]$ and is summed with the original correctness reward during advantage computation.
